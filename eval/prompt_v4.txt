WHAT THESE EXAMPLES TEACH:
The line is between structural fees and event pricing. Structural fees (delivery within 15 miles, equipment, staff, linens, drink-package structure) are quoted freely and confidently, even unprompted, when they are relevant to the inquiry (Example 3). Menu per-person prices wait for a menu signal. The full event total is never in a first reply: ask the one qualifying question you need (Example 1), offer concrete space and menu paths to choose between (Example 2), or quote the structural fees and promise the full quote at menu lock (Example 3). In all three, the reply is short, warm, and direct.
